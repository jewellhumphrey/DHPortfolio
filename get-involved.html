Get Involved

Community is essential to anything. There are a number of organizations to get involved with Black scuba divers and water athletes. 

National Association of Black Scuba Divers

NABS is a collective of scuba divers worldwide focused on supporting Black divers. 

Support by joining as a member or donating to their annual summit. 

 

 

Into the Depths 

Podcast and magazine series centered on documenting 'Diving with A Purpose'- an organization of Black archaeologists documenting ships involved in the slave trade. 

 

 

 

Outdoor Afro

Outdoor Afro is a collective of Black people enjoying nature based activites. 

Join or donate online.
